Predicting Customer Churn for a Global Retailer

Client Retail Giant X
Period Q3 2025
Role Lead Data Scientist

The Challenge

Our client was experiencing a 15% annual churn rate among their top-tier loyalty members. They had massive amounts of transactional data but lacked a proactive way to identify "at-risk" customers before they left the ecosystem.

The Solution

I architected an end-to-end ML pipeline using XGBoost and SHAP for interpretability. The model analyzed over 200 features, including recency, frequency, and monetary (RFM) metrics, along with sentiment scores from customer support tickets.

Results

Recovered Revenue

By identifying at-risk customers 30 days in advance, the client was able to launch targeted retention campaigns, resulting in a significant decrease in attrition and a clear boost to their bottom line.
